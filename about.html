About Rietta Hotels

Set on picturesque landscaped gardens, our hotel sits at the heart of Nigeria's Federal Capital Territory and Abuja's commercial district. We're less than three kilometers from Abuja National Mosque and Millennium Park, within 20 minutes of the city center. Enjoy our on-site casino, handicraft market, airline kiosks, 24-hour fitness center, and outdoor pool.

Offering an outdoor pool and views of the mountains, Rietta Hotels is set in Abuja. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some units have a seating area for your convenience. Each room comes with a private bathroom equipped with a bath and bidet. There is free shuttle service, a 24-hour front desk, gift shop, and shops at the property. You can play tennis at the hotel. Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport is 16 mi away.
